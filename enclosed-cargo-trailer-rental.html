FAQ
Common questions about the enclosed trailer.
How much fits in a 7×14 enclosed trailer?
A 7×14 with 6'6" interior height holds the contents of a 1–2 bedroom apartment comfortably. Larger homes may need multiple trips or a bigger truck.
Is the trailer lockable?
Yes — the rear ramp door has a locking mechanism. Bring your own padlock for additional security if leaving the trailer unattended overnight.
What hitch do I need?
A 2-5/16" hitch ball, 7-way trailer plug, and a brake controller on your tow vehicle. We have a hitch available if you need one — just mention it when you book.
How much does it cost?
$110/day, $600/week. A $100 refundable deposit is held at booking and returned when the trailer comes back in good condition. Tennessee sales tax applies.
Where do I pick up the trailer?
Pickup is in Ooltewah, TN. The exact address is sent with your confirmation email once your booking is approved — typically within minutes.
Can I book same-day?
Yes — same-day bookings are available when the trailer isn't already reserved. We approve most requests within minutes during business hours.
Do you deliver?
Yes, delivery is available in the Chattanooga area for an additional fee. Call or text (321) 765-3077 to arrange.
