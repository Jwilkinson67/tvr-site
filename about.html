About Us

A local trailer rental company built on simplicity.

Tennessee Valley Rentals is a small, locally owned business based in Ooltewah, TN. We rent well-maintained, fully-equipped trailers to people in the Chattanooga area — no franchises, no upsells, no 800 numbers.

Who we are

TVR was started by Julian Wilkinson with a simple idea: make it easy for people in the Chattanooga area to rent a quality trailer without the hassle of dealing with a big-box rental chain.

We're based in Ooltewah and serve customers across Hamilton and Bradley counties in Tennessee, and into northwest Georgia. Every rental is handled personally — you're not a ticket number here.

We answer the phone. We respond quickly. And if something comes up during your rental, you can reach us directly. That's the difference between renting from a local business and renting from a national chain.
