Privacy Policy
Last updated June 21, 2026

Tennessee Valley Rentals LLC ("TVR," "we," "us") operates rentwithtvr.com and the (423) 395-1377 business phone line. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number provided when you request a quote, book a trailer, call, or text us.
  • Driver's license and proof-of-insurance documents you upload to verify you're eligible to tow.
  • Booking details — trailer selected, rental dates, pickup/return information.
  • Payment is processed directly by Stripe; TVR does not receive or store your full card number.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process, confirm, and manage your rental booking.
  • To verify you're eligible to tow before approving a reservation.
  • To communicate with you about your reservation by email, phone, or text.
  • To respond if you call or text our business line — including an automated text if we miss your call.

Text Messaging

If you call or text (423) 395-1377, you may receive an automated text in direct response (for example, a follow-up text if we miss your call, or a confirmation that we received your text). Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving texts, or HELP for help. Your mobile number and consent are never shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. See our SMS Terms for details.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information — including your phone number — with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We share information only with service providers who help us operate (payment processing, secure data storage, email, and call/text delivery), solely to provide the service you requested.

Data Retention & Security

Booking and verification records are retained as needed to manage your rental and meet legal/insurance obligations, and are stored with industry-standard security practices.
