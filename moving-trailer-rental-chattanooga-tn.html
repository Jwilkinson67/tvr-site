FAQ

Moving trailer questions

How much will fit in a 7×14 enclosed trailer?
A 7×14 comfortably fits the contents of a studio apartment or small 1-bedroom — think 2–3 rooms of furniture plus boxes. For a larger move, you may need multiple trips or a larger truck.
Do I need a special license to tow this?
No special license required. You'll need a vehicle capable of towing (most SUVs and trucks work), a 2-inch hitch ball, and trailer wiring. Not sure if your vehicle qualifies? Call or text us and we'll help you figure it out.
Is the trailer enclosed — will my stuff stay dry?
Yes, fully enclosed with solid aluminum walls and roof. Rain, road spray, and debris stay out. Great for moving anything you don't want exposed to weather.
Can I book same day for a move?
Yes, if the trailer is available. We recommend booking as early as possible — especially for weekend moves when demand is highest. Book online anytime and we'll confirm within minutes.
Where is pickup?
Pickup is in Ooltewah, TN. The exact address is sent in your confirmation email after booking. Convenient from I-75 and Highway 11.
What if I need more than one day?
Multi-day rentals are available. Additional days are charged at the same $110 daily rate. Just let us know when you book how many days you need.
