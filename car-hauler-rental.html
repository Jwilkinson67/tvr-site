FAQ
Common questions about the car hauler.
What vehicles fit on the 7×20 car hauler?
Most standard passenger cars, pickups, and SUVs. The 82" deck width and 20-foot length fit the majority of vehicles. For extra-wide trucks or dually configurations, contact us first to confirm fit.
Can I load a non-running vehicle?
Yes — the included 12,000 lb electric winch is designed specifically for pulling non-running vehicles onto the deck.
What hitch do I need?
A 2-5/16" hitch ball, 7-way trailer plug, and a brake controller on your tow vehicle. We have a hitch available if you need one — just mention it when you book.
How much does it cost?
$100/day, $550/week. A $100 refundable deposit is held at booking and returned when the trailer comes back in good condition. Tennessee sales tax applies.
Where do I pick up the trailer?
Pickup is in Ooltewah, TN. The exact address is sent with your confirmation email once your booking is approved — typically within minutes.
Can I book same-day?
Yes — same-day bookings are available when the trailer isn't already reserved. We approve most requests within minutes during business hours.
Do you deliver?
Yes, delivery is available in the Chattanooga area for an additional fee. Call or text (321) 765-3077 to arrange.
