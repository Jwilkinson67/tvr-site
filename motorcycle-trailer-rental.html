FAQ
Motorcycle trailer questions.
Does the trailer have a wheel chock for motorcycles?
Yes — there's a built-in motorcycle wheel chock that locks the front wheel upright without strapping the bike to the walls. You still use straps to secure it, but the chock does the heavy lifting of keeping it from tipping.
Can I load the bike by myself?
Most riders can load solo using the rear ramp. For heavier touring bikes, having a second person to guide is helpful. The ramp has a gentle angle and the aluminum surface grips well.
Will my Harley fit?
Yes — the 7-foot interior width and 6'6" height accommodate most Harley-Davidson models including touring bikes like the Road Glide and Street Glide. Very tall ape hangers may need a measurement check first.
What hitch do I need on my tow vehicle?
A 2-5/16" hitch ball, 7-way trailer plug, and a brake controller. We have a hitch available if your vehicle needs one — just mention it when you book.
How much does it cost?
$110/day, $600/week. A $100 refundable deposit is held at booking and returned when the trailer is back in good condition. Tennessee sales tax applies.
Can I book same-day?
Yes — same-day bookings are available when the trailer isn't already reserved. We approve most requests within minutes during business hours.
Where is pickup?
Ooltewah, TN — just east of Chattanooga. The exact address is in your confirmation email once we approve your booking.
